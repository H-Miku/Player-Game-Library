About Dota 2

Dota 2 is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Valve Corporation. As the sequel to Defense of the Ancients (DotA), it features intense 5v5 matches where teams, the Radiant and the Dire, compete to destroy each other’s Ancient. Players choose from over 120 unique heroes, each with distinct abilities and roles, requiring teamwork, strategy, and quick decision-making. With a deep item system, frequent updates, and a thriving esports scene—including The International, one of the largest esports tournaments—Dota 2 remains one of the most competitive and influential games in the genre.
